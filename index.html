About Me

I will graduate in Fall 2025 from the University of Wisconsin–Eau Claire with a Bachelor of Science in Geographic Information Systems & Technology and a certificate in Urban & Regional Planning. I am interested in applying GIS to real-world problems involving urban development, transportation networks, and environmental analysis.

A lot of my recent work focuses on automating geospatial tasks, building repeatable workflows, and improving the efficiency of spatial analysis. I have a strong background using ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and other Esri tools to manage data, create maps, and support planning and environmental projects throughout my time at UW–Eau Claire.

  • Network analysis and route optimization
