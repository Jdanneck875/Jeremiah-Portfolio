Capstone Project: Route Optimization for Beverage Distribution

For my Geography capstone project, I developed a complete route optimization workflow for Lee Beverage of Wisconsin. The goal of the project was to improve the efficiency of beverage delivery operations by automating geocoding, routing, and map production using ArcGIS Pro and scripting.

This work brought together spatial data management, network analysis, and cartography to compare manually planned routes with optimized GIS-generated routes. The final workflow is repeatable, allowing the company to analyze new delivery data over time.

Project Goals

  • Automate the geocoding of delivery stop addresses.
  • Create and maintain a routable transportation network for analysis.
  • Generate optimized routes using R.
  • Compare optimized routes to existing manual delivery routes.
  • Produce clear, professional map outputs and summaries.

What I Learned

Completing this capstone project strengthened my ability to use GIS for real-world problem solving. I gained hands-on experience working with raw operational datasets, building automated workflows, and designing maps and charts that can support decision-making for an organization.

I also developed a deeper understanding of network analysis, geocoding, and spatial data management, and I improved my ability to communicate GIS results to people who may not work with spatial data every day.
